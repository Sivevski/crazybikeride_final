Tour:Vienna-Split

Day 7:Trieste-Porozina(Cres Island)

From the camp to the city center took me a downhill road without moving the pedals. It was sunny early morning on 26.05.2018.The plan: drink Espresso in Trieste and head  through Istra all the way down to the Adriatic coast where i would take the ferry to Cres Island. I will also mention that the original plan was to ride a long the west coast of Istra and cross the cities of Pula and Rovinj but due to time constraints  the plan was changed with a shorter one.
Who doesn’t enjoy a good espresso on the sunny square of an Italian city? Italy has its magic with the relaxed atmosphere ,positive people ,good food and wine.

Crossing Istra was enjoyable for the most of its part. The final 20-30km until the Brestova port were hard. I was dehydrated, hungry and desperately wanted to see the sea expecting than once i arrive in the islands there will be no more mountains .I had to speed up and arrive in the port until 8 p.m. because that was the time when the last ferry to the island of Cres departed. I arrived there 10 minutes before 20 p.m. happy to be  just on time.
Once on the ferry, i met two American guys who were also making Island hoping and spent the night and next day with them. We found a hidden and remote beach near Merag city, and installed our tents.
